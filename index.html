Who We Are

Driving Industrialization in Ghana

Our Story

Located in Dompim No. 1, Narubiz sits at the forefront of agricultural transformation. We recently integrated a solar PV system to ensure sustainable operations.

Leadership

A 100% Ghanaian-owned enterprise founded by three local rubber farmers. Led by Managing Director Emmanuel Akwasi Owusu and Director of Operations Capt. James Owusu-Koranteng.

1D1F + Launch

A proud participant in the One District One Factory initiative. The factory was officially commissioned in September 2021 by President Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo.

ESG Commitment
